# Rooms & Restoration

Every restorable room in the mountain follows the same four-stage arc. A room is more meaningful than a stat — restoring *places*, not accumulating numbers, is meant to be the primary unit of visible progress.

| Stage | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Ruined** | Collapsed, rubble-filled, barely interactable |
| **Cleared** | Rubble removed, shape and purpose visible, basic function possible |
| **Restored** | Working as intended, full function unlocked |
| **Masterwork** | Peak state — rare, usually funded by True-metals |

Each stage change must be visibly different on screen, not just a bigger number under the hood.

---

## The Stockpile *(East)*

| Stage | Cost | Insight | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cleared | 20 Wood, 5 Copper Ingot | 300 | Manual ore storage; drills can deposit automatically |
| Restored | 20 Copper Ingot, 10 Iron Ingot, 30 Wood | 800 | Stockpile ×3 capacity; Narag-Bund hauls drill ore here automatically |
| Masterwork (The Deep Archive) | 30 Iron Ingot, 3 True Copper | 2,000 | Stockpile ×10 capacity; shows ore/min rate on the Mountain Console |

## The Trade Hall *(South)*

Requires restoration score 2,000 to unlock at all.

| Stage | Cost | Insight | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cleared | 25 Wood, 10 Copper Ingot, 3 Iron Ingot | 500 | Merchant arrives every 10 min; trade cut gems for rare materials |
| Restored | 15 Iron Ingot, 20 Copper Ingot, 30 Wood | 1,200 | Merchant every 5 min; expanded inventory |
| Masterwork (The Great Exchange) | 30 Iron Ingot, 3 True Copper, 1 True Iron | 3,000 | Merchant always present; exotic goods including Cave Fern spores |

See [The Trade Hall](Trade-Hall.md) for what's actually on offer.

## The Deep Foundry *(Northwest)*

Requires restoration score 4,000 to unlock at all.

| Stage | Cost | Insight | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cleared | 20 Iron Ingot, 20 Wood, 5 Deepstone Ingot | 1,000 | Deepstone tool recipes; better smelt success rate |
| Restored | 40 Iron Ingot, 15 Deepstone Ingot, 2 True Iron | 2,500 | Iron Drill buildable; Hearthsap smelting |
| Masterwork (The Grand Forge) | 30 Deepstone Ingot, 2 True Deepstone | 5,000 | Starstone recipes *(pending Starstone content)*; Drill Bit tool; max forge yield |

## The Archive *(North)*

Requires restoration score 6,000 to unlock at all — the deepest room in the current game.

| Stage | Cost | Insight | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cleared | 3 True Copper, 2 True Iron, 10 Ironwood | 2,000 | Console upgrade: full lore display |
| Restored | 5 True Iron, 2 True Deepstone, 20 Ironwood | 5,000 | Insight gain +20% permanently |
| Masterwork (The Deep Record) | 5 True Deepstone | 10,000 | The Narrator speaks as the mountain itself |
