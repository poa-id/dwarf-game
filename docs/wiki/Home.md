# The Hearth & The Deep — Wiki

*A restoration game. One dwarf. One dying mountain. Everything you do — mining, smithing, planting, tending the fire — is in service of bringing it back to life.*

This wiki documents everything currently in the game: skills, crafting, automation, rooms, and the mountain itself. It's written for players who want a reference, not a walkthrough — nothing here is more of a "spoiler" than what you'd find by exploring and reading tooltips, except where marked.

> **Spoiler note:** A few things in this game are deliberately hidden from you until you're close to discovering them anyway (certain upgrades, one companion, some room contents). Pages that touch on these are marked with a **⚠ Spoiler** banner near the relevant section. Skip those sections if you'd rather find them yourself.
