# The Trade Hall

Cut gems are the **only** currency the merchant accepts. Everything on offer is something the mountain cannot produce itself — foreign seeds, rare materials, exotic fuels — nothing duplicates what you can already make yourself.

The Trade Hall must first be unlocked and restored — see [Rooms & Restoration](Rooms-and-Restoration.md) for the stage costs. Once cleared, a merchant begins arriving on a schedule that gets more frequent as the room is restored further:

| Room stage | Merchant frequency |
|---|---|
| Cleared | every 10 minutes |
| Restored | every 5 minutes |
| Masterwork | always present |

## Offers by room stage

### Cleared

| Give | Receive | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 2 Cut Quartz | 1 Cave Fern Spore | "From caves further east. Grows fast in still air." |
| 5 Cut Quartz | 1 Ancient Seed | "A slow-growing tree. Worth the wait." |
| 2 Cut Garnet | 2 Hearthsap | "Already rendered. Foreign kiln-work." |

### Restored

| Give | Receive | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 4 Cut Garnet | 1 Ancient Heartwood Seed | "Three-hour tree. Bears gemstones as fruit, the merchant swears." |
| 1 Cut Amethyst | 5 Ironwood | "Cut from felled trees in the valley. Dense as iron." |
| 3 Cut Amethyst | 1 Deepstone Ingot | "Smelted in a working deep-forge. The merchant asks no questions." |

### Masterwork

| Give | Receive | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 8 Cut Amethyst | 1 True Copper | "Purified far away. He does not reveal the process." |
| 12 Cut Amethyst | 1 True Iron | "Rarer still. A dwarf who trades for this hasn't earned it — but might use it." |

These are the only reliable sources of True-metals outside the [Smelter's](Smelter-and-True-Metals.md) low-percentage drop chance.
